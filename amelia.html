About me

Amelia Narduzzo

I am Amelia narduzzo a 15 year old girl from Toronto. One major moment in my life was when I became a member of the National Team of my swim club, Toronto Swim Club. I had to improve my time- management skills because I was not used to missing a large amount of school. I was able to persevere through this by creating a calendar where I would write all of my upcoming assignments.

I am a creative person who constantly is finding new ways to improve places around me. For example I enjoy thinking of ways to make my school, The Bishop Strachan School, a better environment for the students. I am taking entrepreneurship this year which helped me find ways to make the school become a better place.
