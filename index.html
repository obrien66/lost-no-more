Lost no more

Lost No More was designed to help people keep track of personal belongings. The product is comprised of a dongle that can fit on a keychain or in a wallet (wherever you need it) and an app for your smartphone. The dongle communicates with the app via bluetooth to find relative and global position. This is important because, just by opening the app, you can learn where you've left your belongings. With this product, people will save money, waste less time, and stay more relaxed because of the reliable technologies that keep their belongings within reach.
